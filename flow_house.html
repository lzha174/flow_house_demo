Flow House is a powerful discrete event simulation tool designed to optimize complex processes involving concurrent events. It enables precise modeling of real-world concurrent tasks to improve efficiency and decision quality.

This framework is an enhanced version of my earlier project, HealthCare Acute Flow.

‚ö° What Flow House Can Optimize

Roster Optimization

Optimize staff allocation across shifts to ensure the right mix of skills are available at the right times.

Job Arrival Optimization

Test different job arrival patterns to balance workload and reduce bottlenecks.

Workflow Strategy Optimization

Experiment with alternative job workflows and transitions to improve overall efficiency.

Stress Testing

Use synthetic data to simulate high-demand scenarios and assess system robustness.

üöÄ Major Improvements

Multi-Resource Support

Jobs can request multiple staff members with different skills. Previously, jobs could only be handled by one staff at a time.

Smarter Job Dispatch

Dispatch logic has been improved to account for multi-resource support.

Petri Net with Snakes

Flow logic is built using the snakes library instead of pm4py, improving usability and flexibility.
